Classic Homemade Lasagna

A rich, meaty sauce and a creamy ricotta-mozzarella filling make this classic lasagna an absolute crowd-pleaser. Perfect for family dinners!

Prep Time: 30 mins
Cook Time: 1 hour
Yields: 8-10 servings

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb bulk Italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 (24 oz) jars of marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. 1. Boil Noodles: Cook the lasagna noodles in salted boiling water according to package directions. Drain and lay flat on foil.
  2. 2. Make Meat Sauce: In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, sausage, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is browned. Drain excess fat, then stir in the marinara sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. 3. Prepare Cheese Mixture: In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese.
  4. 4. Layer the Lasagna: Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Add a layer of 4 noodles. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then 1 cup of meat sauce, and 1 cup of mozzarella. Repeat these layers twice. Top with remaining noodles, meat sauce, and all remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  5. 5. Bake: Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 15 minutes until the top is bubbling and golden brown. Let cool for 15 minutes before slicing.
