Sweet Shortcrust Pastry

Prep time: 15 mins
Chill time: 1 hour
Yields: 1 pie crust

Ingredients

  • 1 $\frac{1}{2}$ cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • $\frac{1}{4}$ cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • $\frac{1}{4}$ tsp salt
  • $\frac{1}{2}$ cup (115g) unsalted butter, cold & cubed
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2-3 tbsp ice-cold water

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. Rub the cold, cubed butter into the dry ingredients using your fingertips until it resembles coarse sand.
  3. Add the egg yolk and 1 tablespoon of ice water. Mix until the dough comes together, adding more water a teaspoon at a time if too dry.
  4. Form the dough into a flat disc, wrap tightly in plastic wrap, and chill for at least 1 hour before rolling.
