Classic French Toast

Prep time: 5 mins | Cook time: 10 mins | Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 8 thick slices of bread (brioche or challah work best)
  • 4 large eggs
  • $\frac{1}{2}$ cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter (for the pan)
  • Maple syrup and powdered sugar (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Mix the custard: In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and cinnamon.
  2. Heat the pan: Melt the butter in a large skillet or non-stick griddle over medium heat.
  3. Soak the bread: Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture. Let it soak for about 2-3 seconds per side so it absorbs the liquid but doesn't get soggy.
  4. Cook: Place the soaked bread into the hot skillet. Cook for 3-4 minutes per side, or until golden brown.
  5. Serve: Serve hot immediately, dusted with powdered sugar and generously drizzled with maple syrup.
